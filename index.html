Scabulous Helps You Cheat At Scrabulous

My wife keeps beating me at Scrabulous, so I wrote this page.

Example uses:

  • Type B..Y.. into the pattern box and click the button to find every 5-letter word with a B at the beginning and Y in the middle. You will get a list with all sorts of weird but legal words, like:
    • BARYES
    • BUNYES
  • If you only want to see words you can play with your tiles, then type your tiles into the tiles box, and your pattern in the pattern box, and then click the button.
